About

Hi, I'm Prem.

I'm a freelance photographer based in Hyderabad, Telangana, with over six years of experience across portrait, travel, candid and event photography. My approach favours natural light, genuine moments and a quiet, observational style over heavily directed shoots.

I've held leadership roles in photography communities including KL Samyak and Surabhi, where I helped mentor emerging photographers and organise collaborative shoots. Today, I work with individuals, couples and organisations across India who want photography that feels honest rather than staged.
